# Decision Engine

## Purpose

Combine all analysis into one investment decision.

## Inputs

- Business Score
- Industry Score
- Moat Score
- Management Score
- Financial Score
- Valuation Score

## Outputs

- Investment Score
- Recommendation
- Confidence
- Risks
- Opportunities

## Recommendation

90-100 : Strong Buy

80-89 : Buy

65-79 : Watchlist

50-64 : Avoid

Below 50 : Reject
